How to Install in Outlook

Why two signatures? Outlook Desktop does not automatically resize signatures for mobile devices. To ensure your signature looks perfect on phones, you must create a separate "Mobile" signature in your Outlook Mobile App settings.
  1. Copy Desktop: Click the black "Copy Desktop Signature" button above.
  2. Paste in Outlook (PC/Mac): Go to File > Options > Mail > Signatures. Create a new signature named "Quantira Desktop" and paste (Ctrl+V).
  3. Copy Mobile: Click the blue "Copy Mobile Signature" button above.
  4. Paste in Outlook Mobile App: Open the Outlook app on your phone. Go to Settings (Gear Icon) > Signature. Paste the mobile version there.
